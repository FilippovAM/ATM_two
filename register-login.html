What sets us apart?

research and advice

Independant

Our research and advice is completely independent in every sense. We are not paid by any of the companies that we recommend in our research reports as our members’ interests always come first. We do not provide recommendations simply to generate transaction fees or commissions, as we do not physically hold the assets of our members.

absolute returns

performance

At ATM we believe performance implies making money (positive absolute returns), while the fund management industry is focused on beating benchmarks and may charge investors performance fees even when fund loses money (as it outperformed the benchmark)

We invest with you

Confidence

Senior Partners at ATM all follow their own advice, we would not make a recommendation to our members unless we would also be comfortable putting our own money into the idea. All research analysts have significant ‘skin in the game’ through personal investment alongside our members.
